# ClearForge Technology Lexicon

### Spectra  
Telemetry and event bus collecting metrics, logs, and traces across the platform.  
**Purpose:** provide total observability and traceability.

### Prism  
Observability and analytics visualization layer.  
**Purpose:** turn Spectra’s telemetry into dashboards, alerts, and insights.

### ForgeNarrate  
Narrative intelligence engine translating system events into contextual reports for each persona.  
**Purpose:** data → story → decision.

### Presidium Gateway  
Privacy mesh built on Presidio; strips and reconstitutes PII according to role and policy.  
**Purpose:** privacy-by-design at wire speed.

### Aurora Stack  
AI orchestration stack for multi-LLM routing and consensus.  
**Purpose:** optimize accuracy, latency, and cost dynamically.

### CoreForge  
Governance and CI/CD engine enforcing RBAC, ABAC, and policy-as-code.  
**Purpose:** compliance without bureaucracy.

### ReadyRank  
Conversational Node.js web builder and ASO optimization engine.  
**Purpose:** automate digital discoverability.
